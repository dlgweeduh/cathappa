December 19th @ UTM - CCT Building

Get the Appathon Guide

What are we gonna do?!

Create Awesomeness

You'll be using cutting-edge Web Tools to build a functional (and beautiful) Web App to tackle Youth Homelessness!

Develop Super-Powers

True, building Web Apps is not the same as being able to fly or read minds... but we like to think they are.

Empower Peers

We'll turn our brilliant minds to support our Catholic partners, Covenant House, along with our friends & family who are having difficulties at home.

Who's gonna be there?

Covenant House

Covenant House is a leader in helping young people who are experiencing troubles at home. Suzie from C.H. will be presenting on the issue!

U of T Professors

The University of Toronto (Mississauga) is hosting us, and they'll be judging our apps! Who knows, you might be pitching your app to your future prof!

DP Catholic Teachers

Well of course they have to be there, right? But don't expect too much (if any) help from your them: this Appathon challenges you to learn and build on your own!

DP Catholic Students

More than 200 of us! The brightest CS minds will compete to build the best app in a (ludicrously) short amount of time!

DP Volunteers

We'll have a bunch of amazing Dufferin Peel volunteers who'll help keep all the chaos intact!

U of T Students

They'll be helping us get to work (WiFi, power outlets, all that fun stuff), and judging our apps, too!

What should my team aim for?

Most Technical

  • Was your hack hard to make, was it complex?
  • How feasible is your project?
  • Will your project work in the real world?
  • How well the demo works

Best Design

  • How elegant/ aesthetically pleasing the project is
  • How well the demo has been developed -- did you think about all aspects of it?

Most Creative

  • How unique is your idea, has it already been executed?
  • Does the idea promote Catholic Social Good?
  • Will the idea benefit Homeless Youth?
